Cruelty-Free Commitment

100% Cruelty-Free

At LUNOR, we believe that beauty should never come at the expense of our furry friends. We are proud to be a 100% cruelty-free brand. None of our products or ingredients are tested on animals, and we never will.

No Animal Testing

We strictly prohibit animal testing at every stage of product development, from ingredients to final formulations.

Ethical Sourcing

Our ingredients are responsibly sourced from suppliers who share our commitment to cruelty-free practices.

Third-Party Certified

We are certified by Leaping Bunny, the gold standard for cruelty-free products worldwide.

"Beautiful skincare shouldn't harm any living creature. That's our promise to you and to animals everywhere."
